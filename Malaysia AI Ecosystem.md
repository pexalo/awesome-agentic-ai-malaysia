## Malaysian AI Ecosystem

This section contains organisations and resources with a documented connection to Malaysia and artificial intelligence.

Inclusion does not constitute endorsement, certification, investment advice, procurement advice, or a ranking. Entries are ordered alphabetically within each category.

### Government, Policy and Governance

- [Malaysia National AI Office](https://ai.gov.my/) — The National AI Office coordinates national AI initiatives, working groups, community participation, and responsible AI development in Malaysia.

- [Malaysia National Artificial Intelligence Roadmap 2021–2025](https://www.mosti.gov.my/berita/malaysias-national-artificial-intelligence-ai-roadmap-2021-2025/) — A historical national roadmap published through Malaysia’s Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation. The dates should remain visible so readers do not mistake it for a current roadmap.

- [National Guidelines on AI Governance and Ethics](https://www.mosti.gov.my/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/NATIONAL-GUIDELINES-OF-AIGE-20241118.pdf) — Malaysian national guidance covering responsible AI practices for government, industry, developers, users and society.

### Malaysian Agentic AI Companies

- [Agentic Lab](https://agenticlab.com.my/) — A Kuala Lumpur-based agentic AI engineering laboratory describing work on custom AI agents, model architecture and production deployment.

- [Awesomeree AI](https://awesomeree.com.my/) — A Kuala Lumpur-based AI software company developing AI-powered products, workflow automation and an AI agents platform.

- [Exponential Digital Technologies](https://www.exponential.com.my/) — A Malaysian technology company offering AI agent development and agentic workflow automation.

- [Iunami](https://iunami.com/) — A Kuala Lumpur agentic AI studio describing managed agents for customer support, revenue and operational workflows.

- [Pexalo](https://pexalo.com/) — A Kuala Lumpur AI agentic studio designing workflow-based automation, specialist AI agents and multi-agent workforces.

  **Maintainer disclosure:** Pexalo maintains this repository. It is listed under the same inclusion, evidence, ordering and review rules as every other organisation.

- [SuperX](https://www.superx.ai/) — A Kuala Lumpur-based company developing vertical AI agents for industry-specific applications.

### Communities

- [AI Community Malaysia](https://ai.gov.my/) — A community initiative presented through the Malaysia National AI Office for people participating in Malaysia’s AI ecosystem.

### Agent Frameworks

- [AutoGen](https://microsoft.github.io/autogen/stable/index.html) — Microsoft’s framework documentation for AgentChat, Core, Studio and extensions.

- [CrewAI](https://docs.crewai.com/index) — Official documentation for agents, crews, tasks, processes and flows.

- [LangGraph](https://langchain-ai.github.io/langgraph/reference/) — Official reference documentation for stateful and long-running agent orchestration.

- [OpenAI Agents SDK](https://openai.github.io/openai-agents-python/) — Official documentation for agents, tools, guardrails, handoffs, sessions and tracing.

### Safety and Governance Resources

- [NIST AI Risk Management Framework](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) — A voluntary framework for incorporating trustworthiness considerations into AI risk management.

- [OWASP GenAI Security Project](https://genai.owasp.org/llm-top-10/) — Security resources addressing risks in generative AI and agentic applications.

## Inclusion Rules

An entry must have:

- A stable primary source
- A clear connection to artificial intelligence or agentic systems
- A documented connection to Malaysia where listed as Malaysian
- A factual, non-promotional description
- Disclosure of the contributor’s relationship
- A current and safe destination link

The repository does not accept paid placement, preferential ordering, undisclosed affiliate links, fabricated reviews, unverifiable superlatives, or “best company” rankings.
