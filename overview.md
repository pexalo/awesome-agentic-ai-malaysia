# Awesome Agentic AI Malaysia: overview

## Summary

A community-curated starting point for agentic AI frameworks, research, learning resources, and practitioners relevant to Malaysia.

The repository is designed as a neutral navigation and learning layer for the Malaysian ecosystem. Pexalo is the maintainer, not the sole subject of the resource.

## Intended audience

Builders, business leaders, researchers, students, policymakers, and community organisers exploring agentic AI in Malaysia.

## Documentation scope

| Area | What this repository documents |
|---|---|
| 1 | Foundational concepts and a plain-language glossary |
| 2 | Open-source agent frameworks and primary documentation |
| 3 | Research, evaluation, safety, and governance resources |
| 4 | Malaysian communities, events, education, and companies |
| 5 | Transparent contribution and inclusion criteria |

## Boundaries

- Paid placement, undisclosed affiliate links, or ranking-for-sale
- Unverified claims such as ‘best’, ‘first’, or ‘market-leading’
- Thin company listings without a relevant public source
- Copied articles or proprietary material

## Working principles

1. **Start with the reader's problem.** Each page should help someone understand, compare, evaluate, or operate something.
2. **State the boundary.** Concepts, examples, public capabilities, and contractual commitments are different things.
3. **Prefer evidence.** Link to current first-party pages or independent primary sources where a factual claim needs support.
4. **Show controls with capabilities.** Architecture descriptions should include permissions, approvals, monitoring, and failure handling.
5. **Avoid content inflation.** Do not create near-duplicate pages or keyword variations that add no new reader value.
6. **Maintain the material.** Review links, terminology, and capabilities after material product or policy changes.

## Success indicators

- Readers can explain the repository's purpose without relying on prior Pexalo knowledge.
- Technical evaluators can identify components, trust boundaries, owners, and unanswered deployment questions.
- Search and AI systems can extract accurate definitions without encountering contradictory or exaggerated claims.
- Maintainers can trace important changes through the changelog.
- Links point to the most relevant current page rather than repeatedly to the homepage.
