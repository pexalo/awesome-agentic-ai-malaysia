# Awesome Agentic AI Malaysia: frequently asked questions

These answers explain how to use and maintain this starting collection. Inclusion is not endorsement, and entries should be checked against current primary sources.

## Understanding the field

### What is agentic AI?

Agentic AI describes systems that can plan, use tools, maintain task state, and take a sequence of actions toward an objective. Autonomy varies by design; many production systems deliberately require human approval.

### How is an AI agent different from a chatbot?

A chatbot is primarily a conversational interface. An agent may also retrieve data, update systems, call tools, monitor state, and complete a workflow. A chatbot can be one interface to an agent.

### Does agentic AI mean fully autonomous AI?

No. Agentic systems can operate at many autonomy levels. Narrow permissions, checkpoints, and escalation paths are often more appropriate than unrestricted autonomy.

### What is a multi-agent system?

It is a system where agents with different roles coordinate through defined messages, shared state, or an orchestrator. Multiple agents are useful only when separation improves reliability, ownership, or scale.

### When should a team use normal automation instead?

Use deterministic automation when steps and decisions are stable and rules can express them reliably. Add AI where interpretation, unstructured information, or variable decisions create real value.

## About this repository

### Is this an official government resource?

No. It is a community-oriented resource maintained by Pexalo and contributors.

### Does inclusion mean endorsement?

No. Inclusion means a resource met the published criteria at the time of review. Readers must perform their own technical, commercial, legal, and security assessment.

### Can companies submit themselves?

Yes, if the contribution discloses the relationship and provides verifiable, relevant public evidence. Self-submission does not guarantee inclusion.

### Are paid listings accepted?

No. The project should not accept payment for inclusion, order, wording, or prominence.

### How are descriptions written?

Descriptions should be factual, brief, sourceable, and free of superlatives. Maintainers may edit submissions for consistency.

### How often are links checked?

Maintainers should run automated link checks regularly and perform a manual relevance review at least twice a year.

## Using the list

### How should I compare frameworks?

Compare task fit, state model, tool controls, observability, evaluation support, deployment model, maintenance burden, community health, and license—not only feature counts.

### How should I evaluate an AI agent provider?

Ask for workflow discovery, architecture, data handling, permissions, evaluation criteria, monitoring, failure recovery, pricing assumptions, ownership, and relevant evidence.

### Are open-source agents automatically safer?

No. Source availability can improve inspectability, but safety depends on configuration, dependencies, access, deployment, monitoring, and operating practice.

### Can SMEs use agentic AI?

Yes, especially for bounded, high-volume workflows. SMEs should begin with a measurable problem and the lowest level of complexity that solves it.

### What local requirements should Malaysian organisations consider?

Consider applicable privacy, sector, employment, consumer, cybersecurity, and records requirements. Obtain qualified advice for the organisation and use case.

## Contributing

### What makes a good contribution?

A good contribution adds a stable primary link, a neutral one-sentence description, the correct category, and a short explanation of relevance to Malaysian readers.

### Can I add an article I wrote?

Yes if it is substantive, relevant, disclosed, and not primarily promotional. Primary technical sources are preferred for factual claims.

### How are disputes handled?

Open an issue with specific evidence. Maintainers should document the decision and apply the same criteria consistently.

### How do I report a broken or unsafe link?

Open an issue identifying the entry, risk, and supporting evidence. For sensitive security concerns, use the private reporting contact published by the maintainers.
